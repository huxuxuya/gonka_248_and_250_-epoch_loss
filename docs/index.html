Why rows appear here

An anomaly means the participant has signed proof data and validation/performance evidence, is not explained by exclusion or downtime, but rewarded_coins is lower than the audit expected reward. It is a technical mismatch to investigate, not a final payout verdict.

Rows explained by downtime or explicit exclusion are included in the audit data but are not shown in the settlement anomaly matrix. For epoch 272, the affected chat addresses are downtime-test failures, so the incident report and participant proof table are the source for that case.
